Salted Caramel Apple Pie

This salted caramel apple pie is as decadent as a dessert can get. It's an impressive end to any meal, but it's absolutely perfect for holiday entertaining.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Unsalted butter
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Granny Smith apples
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Make the filling
  4. Roll the dough
  5. Assemble the pie
  6. Bake the pie
